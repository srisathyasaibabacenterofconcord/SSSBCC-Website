Welcome to the Sri Sathya Sai Baba Center of Concord (SSSBCC)

The Sri Sathya Sai Baba Center of Concord (SSSBCC) was established in the late 1970s under Swami’s direct guidance and is one of the oldest centers in the United States. Located in Contra Costa County in the East Bay Area of California, the center is part of the California Region within Zone 1 of the Sri Sathya Sai Global Council (SSSGC).

The center convenes every Thursday on Zoom for Study Circle discussions, and on the 2nd and 4th Sundays of each month for Devotional, Service, Sri Sathya Sai Educare (SSSE/Balvikas), and Young Adult (YA) programs at a private residence in Concord, CA. For more information, please contact us at ssbc.concord@gmail.com.
