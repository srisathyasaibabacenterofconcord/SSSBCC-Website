Center Programs

Weekly Zoom Meetings

Every Thursday we meet on Zoom at 8:45 PM. Meetings include announcements, a Study Circle where we study and discuss Baba's discourses and literature, followed by Arati and Closing Prayers.

In-Person Meetings

On the 2nd & 4th Sundays of each month, meetings are held in person, usually around noon. Programs include:

  • Devotional: Bhajans (devotional singing)
  • Service: Singing at convalescent homes, serving food at the Concord Adult Homeless Shelter, donating/distributing supplies, preparing and distributing brown bag lunches to homeless people in the East Bay area
  • SSSE/Balvikas: Education in human values classes for children ages 6-18
  • Young Adult (YA) Program: Activities for men and women ages 18-35 involving discussions on Sai literature, community service, and preparing for regional offerings

“Transform work into worship, and worship into wisdom” - Sathya Sai Baba

For meeting details, please send an email to ssbc.concord@gmail.com.
