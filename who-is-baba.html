Who is Sathya Sai Baba?

Sathya Sai Baba (1926–2011) was a spiritual leader and humanitarian from India, known worldwide for his teachings on love, selfless service, and unity of all religions. He inspired millions to live a life of compassion, truth, peace, and righteousness.

His famous teaching: "Love All, Serve All" continues to guide devotees around the globe.
